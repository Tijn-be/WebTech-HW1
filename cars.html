Technical Overview

A Formula 1 car is basically a very expensive science experiment with wings. Everything exists for one reason: go brrrr.

  • Aerodynamics: Wings + floor + diffuser make the car stick to the ground like glue. Great downforce or whatever, it just works.
  • Power Unit: Engine + hybrid system = speed and brrrr sounds. Mostly cool noises. Can't forget about the fairy dust emulsifier, which is the secret sauce that makes our cars go faster than the speed of light. It's like a magic potion, but for cars.
  • Suspension: Keeps the car stable while the driver is doing 300 km/h around corners. It's the cars way of giving the driver a smooth ride while going at speeds that a rocket ship would be lowkey jealous of.
  • Tyres: Soft, Medium, Hard, or none at all, depending on the vibe of the race and how much we want to mess with the other teams. We like to keep them guessing.
