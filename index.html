About Red Bull Racing

Red Bull Racing joined Formula 1 in 2005 and immediately decided "yeah we're here to win" and then kind of just did that. The team is famous for innovation, speed, and occasionally building something that looks illegal but isn't.

Our engineers don't sleep. They just consume knowledge, caffeine, and suspiciously sparkling "fairy dust" (we are legally forbidden to explain what that means).
