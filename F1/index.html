Formula 1

This website follows the current Formula 1 season through the team standings, recent race results, upcoming weekends, driver profiles, and team pages. Instead of acting like a poster, it is meant to give visitors enough context to understand who is competing, where the season stands, and why each page matters.

From the homepage you can move into the constructor table, compare recent results, open full team mini-sites, and browse individual driver pages. Logged-in visitors also see their favourite team highlighted around the site.
