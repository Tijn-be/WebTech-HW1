History

Haas became Formula 1's first American-led new team in decades when it joined the grid in 2016. Since then it has built its identity around close Ferrari technical ties, a lean operating model, and a transatlantic brand presence.

Team Members

Haas depends on a compact but highly specialised structure that includes race engineers, mechanics, strategists, manufacturing support, logistics staff, and data analysts. In Formula 1, even smaller organisations still rely on precise coordination across many roles.

Featured Circuit

Circuit of the Americas

Circuit of the Americas is the clearest modern home-race reference for Haas because it connects the team's American identity with one of the biggest Formula 1 events on the calendar.
