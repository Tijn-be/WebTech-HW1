History

Audi's Formula 1 project grows from the long-running Sauber organisation in Hinwil. Across the past two decades that same base has competed as Sauber, BMW Sauber, Sauber again, and Alfa Romeo or Kick Sauber before transitioning toward the Audi works project.

Team Members

Audi's future race programme depends on the same mix of specialists that every leading Formula 1 team needs: drivers, engineers, strategists, mechanics, analysts, operations staff, and leadership groups that connect the trackside team with the factory programme.

Featured Circuit

Hockenheimring

Hockenheimring is a strong fit for Audi because it reflects the brand's German motorsport heritage and sits naturally alongside the manufacturer identity the team is bringing into Formula 1.
