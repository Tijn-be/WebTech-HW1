History

McLaren is one of Formula 1's most successful teams, with roots going back to Bruce McLaren and a long list of championship-winning eras. The team's modern identity combines historic prestige with a major engineering and technology base in Woking.

Team Members

McLaren's performance is built by far more than the drivers seen on race day. The team depends on engineering groups, aero and design staff, mechanics, pit-crew personnel, strategists, simulator specialists, and support teams working in step from factory to paddock.

Featured Circuit

Silverstone Circuit

Silverstone is a fitting circuit to feature for McLaren because it sits close to the team's British home base and represents the wider environment in which much of Formula 1 operates.
