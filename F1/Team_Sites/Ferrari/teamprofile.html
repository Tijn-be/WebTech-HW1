History

Ferrari is the championship's most iconic and longest continuously competing constructor, with a Formula 1 story stretching back to 1950. The team is closely associated with world titles, legendary drivers, and a racing culture centered on Maranello.

Team Members

Ferrari's Formula 1 operation combines designers, engineers, strategists, mechanics, pit-crew staff, logistics crews, analysts, and commercial teams around one of the most closely watched brands in the sport. Success depends on how those departments work together over a long season.

Featured Circuit

Autodromo Nazionale Monza

Monza is inseparable from Ferrari's Formula 1 identity. It is the team's spiritual home race venue and one of the circuits most strongly linked to Ferrari supporters around the world.
