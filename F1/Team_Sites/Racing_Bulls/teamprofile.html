History

The Racing Bulls team carries forward the Faenza-based organisation once known as Minardi, Toro Rosso, and AlphaTauri. Even as the branding changed, the team remained an important development path for drivers and staff within the wider Red Bull system.

Team Members

Like every Formula 1 organisation, Racing Bulls depends on drivers, race engineers, mechanics, strategists, analysts, aero staff, and logistics teams. Its role as a competitive midfield team also makes efficient coordination especially important.

Featured Circuit

Autodromo Enzo e Dino Ferrari

Imola is a strong match for Racing Bulls because it sits close to the team's Italian base in Faenza and reflects the local motorsport culture tied to the organisation's history.
