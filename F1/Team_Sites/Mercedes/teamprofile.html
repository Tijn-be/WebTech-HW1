History

Mercedes has a layered Formula 1 history, from its original 1950s works effort to its return as a full factory team in 2010 after the Brawn GP takeover. The team then defined the hybrid era with one of the most dominant runs in championship history.

Team Members

Mercedes relies on a broad technical and sporting structure that includes race engineers, aerodynamicists, strategists, mechanics, simulation staff, pit-crew personnel, and factory support teams. That depth is a major part of how front-running Formula 1 teams sustain performance.

Featured Circuit

Silverstone Circuit

Silverstone fits Mercedes well because the team's Formula 1 operation is based in Britain and works within the same high-performance engineering corridor that supports many teams on the grid.
