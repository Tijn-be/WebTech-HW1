History

Cadillac represents a new American Formula 1 project approved to join the grid in 2026. Its story is less about past race results and more about building a competitive works programme, infrastructure, and long-term identity from the ground up.

Team Members

As a new entrant, Cadillac needs every part of a Formula 1 structure to come together quickly: leadership, recruitment, race engineering, aerodynamics, simulation, operations, mechanics, strategy, and commercial support. That behind-the-scenes build is just as important as the driver lineup.

Featured Circuit

Circuit of the Americas

Circuit of the Americas is a natural showcase venue for Cadillac because it represents top-level Formula 1 in the United States and matches the project's American identity.
